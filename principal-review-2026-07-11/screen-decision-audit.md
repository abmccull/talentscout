# Major-Screen Decision Audit

This audit asks whether each major screen creates a legible decision rather than merely displaying a system. Unavailable Early Access paths are marked as controlled/code-traced rather than normally played.

## Screen-by-screen assessment

| Screen / entry | Decision being made | Options, trade-offs and opportunity cost | Uncertainty and information quality | Consequence and feedback | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Main Menu** | Start, continue or load a career | Youth Career is primary; Continue/Load secondary; other specializations are not implied as available here | Clear Early Access/Youth scope | Immediate route transition | **Good gateway.** Strong hierarchy and honest product boundary. |
| **New Game — Identity** | Define scout name, nationality and appearance | Cosmetic identity choices; no gameplay opportunity cost | Sufficient | Persistent identity/portrait | **Complete but mostly cosmetic.** Missing H1/main semantics. |
| **New Game — Specialization** | Select professional lens | Youth is selectable; First Team, Regional and Data are visibly unavailable | Descriptions suggest distinct fantasies, but only one can be tested | Sets primary specialization/perk | **Honest Early Access gate.** Replay choice is not yet real. |
| **New Game — Skills** | Allocate starting competence | Trade points among scouting skills; raises accuracy/access/formulas | Numeric effects are described more than demonstrated; dominant allocations are not compared | Persistent skill profile and later course/perk interactions | **Functional but opaque.** Needs “this changes what evidence you notice” examples. |
| **New Game — Starting Position** | Freelance or club employment | Freelance selectable; club start disabled | Boundary clear | Creates independent finances and access | **Honest but no real path choice.** |
| **New Game — World / country** | Choose starting football world and coverage | Initial country/countries and seed; geography affects pool/access/cost | Regional strategic differences are weakly surfaced | Generates clubs/players/contacts/knowledge | **Meaningful setup with shallow differentiation.** |
| **New Game — Difficulty / scenario / review** | Select risk modifiers and confirm start | Difficulty and optional scenario alter rules/objectives | Scenario predicates and modifiers are not trustworthy; specific effects need previews | Persistent scenario/difficulty; achievements/objectives | **Partially informed.** Some advertised effects are inert or loosely validated. |
| **Dashboard / Desk** | Decide what to do next | Planner, messages, cases and unlocked systems compete; season timeline/status dominate | Urgency, evidence gaps and opportunity deadlines are not primary | Links out; achievement toasts and summaries | **Display-heavy.** Should become the action agenda, not a statistic/timeline collage. |
| **Calendar / Planner** | Allocate the week's scarce time | Observation contexts, follow-up, networking, study, travel, rest; breadth/depth/income/fatigue trade-offs | Cards show cost/duration/unlock, but expected information gain, deadline, obligation and competitive heat are weak | Scheduled activity, fatigue, cost and weekly results | **Core meaningful decision with poor interaction.** Tiny controls, duplicate context cards and no portfolio comparison. |
| **Week Simulation** | React to each day's event and focus | Cast wide, focus target, build context and activity variants | Copy implies differences; several options share flat/default effects and target propagation can differ | Day outcome, observations, IP/XP/messages; weekly summary | **Partly false choice.** Needs validated previews and activity-specific consequences. |
| **Observation Session** | Allocate attention and interpret moments | Player × lens × phase; flag moments, note, change focus, proceed | Strongest uncertainty surface: weather, venue, vague/detail moments, ranges, hidden character | Evidence/knowledge, flags, notes, IP and reflection | **Best current screen.** Meaningful scarcity and immediate interpretation, undermined by downstream count math. |
| **Insight Panel** | Spend scarce IP to accelerate or redirect inference | Actions cost 20–30 IP and vary by specialization | Eligibility/cost shown, but unavailable action state was wrong and results are partly discarded | Intended reveals/rerolls/knowledge effects | **Misleading.** At 0 IP actions appeared enabled and failed silently. Hide or fully connect. |
| **Reflection** | Decide what the observation meant and what to remember | Add note/hypothesis, review flags/conditions, label outlook | Good contextual recap; no diagnostic prompt about contradiction or next test | Journal/note persists to dossier | **Promising but incomplete.** Hypotheses never resolve and next-step selection is weak. |
| **Youth Hub / Prospects** | Triage young players and open follow-up work | Search/filter/select/watch/report/observe | Shows ranges, age, context and status; inherits corrupt confidence counts | Watchlist/navigation/planner targeting | **Useful worklist.** Should become a saved view of one Player Intelligence workspace. |
| **Discoveries / Player Database / Alumni** | Find players by status and inspect history | Filters and overlapping categories | Broad data, but list identity and evidence freshness/fit comparisons are fragmented | Mostly navigation/display | **Overlapping information screens.** Consolidate into views: leads, active cases, reported, signed, alumni, lost. |
| **Player Profile / Dossier** | Decide whether to watch, follow up, compare or report | Watchlist, network tap, report, negotiation/loan paths as unlocked | Rich ranges, notes, reports, traits, status; hidden truth mostly protected | Launches work and shows durable state | **Strong drill-down with overload.** 2,300+ lines, fragmented case history and false appearance/confidence inputs. |
| **Report Writer** | Decide what advice to give and how strongly | Select/remove strengths/weaknesses, summary, conviction | Evidence snippets exist; no audience, need, role, finance, risk plan, alternative or deadline | Craft/price preview, submission, reputation and marketplace | **Core decision is too shallow and economically distorted.** Conviction is monetized more than calibrated. |
| **Report History / Marketplace** | Review, list, price and sell advice | Exclusive/nonexclusive, price, accept/decline bids | Values differ from writer; buyer need/fit and conflict-of-interest context are limited | Cash, listing, delayed evaluation | **Functional market with unreliable feedback.** Manual resubmission and incomplete ledger create dominant exploits. |
| **Inbox / Narrative Event** | Respond to urgent opportunities or consequences | Read, navigate, choose event response, mark complete | “Action required” is not guaranteed to map to a real command; background/urgent items mix | Message/narrative effects, sometimes only copy | **Notification-heavy.** Must become validated Action Inbox plus case/world feed. |
| **Network / Contacts** | Invest a week in access/trust/intel or call in a relationship | Meeting choices and contact actions | Contact type/meter visible; goals, obligations and conflicts weak; gossip data disconnected | Relationship number, tips/messages | **Functional but shallow.** Generic copy and no episodic memory make choices cosmetic. |
| **Rivals** | Monitor/contest another scout's lead | View activity, counter or concede | Rival traits/budget/activity appear; real mandate/cost/state is absent | Messages/activity statistics; counter does not transfer/pay | **Placeholder and misleading.** Hide transactional choices until they use the real lifecycle. |
| **World / International / Country Popup** | Decide whether geography merits money and absence | Book costly multi-week travel; later take assignments | Country cost, duration and knowledge visible; opportunity/deliverable comparison weak | Location/knowledge/access; assignment rewards | **Travel has a real trade-off.** Assignments are timers and displayed date range is ambiguous. |
| **Free Agents** | Identify and potentially recommend/negotiate for available players | Filter, inspect, offer/track | Contract/geography/ability data broad | Contract/club/player state | **Engine-functional, role-confused.** Scout authority versus club authority needs explicit mandate. |
| **Transfer Negotiation** | Make/adjust/walk away from an offer | Fee/add-ons/response | Financial terms visible; client need and scout authority inconsistent | Transfer and finances | **Wrong-role risk.** Appropriate only for a recruitment leader/authorized agent, not every scout. |
| **Loan workflow** | Recommend development move, monitor or recall | Club, rationale, duration, monitoring/recall | Some fit/development data; minutes/level model currently false | Loan deal and reports | **Functional but causally weak.** Natural return/recall race and false match participation damage outcomes. |
| **Career Overview** | Choose development focus, perk/path/job and respond to career status | Perks, job offers, path, tabs for record/finances | Tier gates readable; raw IDs and internal state drift; stage responsibilities unclear | Unlocks, salary/employment, reputation | **Readable progression, weak profession change.** Exact-text E2E drift and impossible transitions confirm fragility. |
| **Equipment** | Spend cash on incremental modifiers | Buy/sell/equip within budget | Costs/effects visible | Cash and scouting bonuses | **Complete but linear.** Mostly obvious upgrades; should support methods/play styles or be simplified. |
| **Training** | Spend money/time to raise skill or unlock course | Course choice and delay | Effects/gates visible | Skills and career eligibility | **Functional but shallow.** Little learning trade-off beyond price/time. |
| **Finance** | Manage solvency, expenses and growth | Purchases, lifestyle, debt, agency costs; inspect history | Balance visible but ledger incomplete | Cash and some history | **Misleading audit surface.** A £600 report-related increase appeared with “No income recorded.” |
| **Agency** *(controlled/code-traced)* | Build office, hire/assign staff, pitch clients, manage payroll | Staff/office/client/international choices | Many numbers; employee market, retention, ethics and quality causality thin | Delegated output and expenses | **Engine ahead of playable path.** £1 salary exploit and discarded analyst boost remove managerial meaning. |
| **NPC Management** *(controlled/code-traced)* | Delegate coverage and review reports | Assign scout/player/territory; accept/review output | Skill and assignment context visible | Scaled observations/reports | **One of the better late-game flows**, but inherits shallow report/evidence outcome model. |
| **Board / Manager meetings** *(controlled/code-traced)* | Spend political capital to defend work or improve satisfaction | Meet/present/directive actions | Meters visible; action cost/cooldown and stakeholder agenda weak | Satisfaction/job security | **False/repeatable decisions.** Free boosts and discarded directive make meetings non-political. |
| **Analytics** | Understand performance and decide where to improve | Filter/read charts | Numerous derived counts; root causes and invalid inputs not exposed | Mostly informational | **Display screen.** Convert charts into linked questions: which bias, stale case, weak region, value miss or client pattern needs action? |
| **Standings / Season Timeline** | Understand football context and target clubs/players | Filter/read | Tables/fixtures look authoritative | Currently display/history only; promotion membership false | **Misleading information screen.** Must not imply consequences until competition state is structural. |
| **Season Awards** | Review notable season outcomes | Dismiss/continue | Broad results, limited drill-down | Some reputation/history, much ephemeral | **Presentation, not decision.** Link every award to durable entity/season records. |
| **Achievements / Hall of Fame** | Track goals and celebrate career | Filter/read; legacy actions later | Global local-storage state can leak between careers | Toast/unlock/Steam/legacy | **Untrustworthy celebration.** Separate save milestones, account achievements and legacy. |
| **Legacy / New Game+** | Retire and carry selected identity into a new career | Choose perks/scenarios | First completion is circular | Intended career archive/new start | **Unreachable/incomplete.** Hide until retirement path and migration are correct. |
| **Save / Load** | Preserve, resume, replace or delete a career | Slots, autosave, provider/cloud choices | Local metadata clear; provider status/conflicts and mid-session loss weak | Full-state Dexie save; Steam write mirror | **Locally functional, cloud-misleading.** Need schema version, sync status, conflict/delete parity and safe checkpoint semantics. |
| **Settings** | Adapt presentation, audio, text and controls | Display/audio/accessibility options | Clear categories | Local settings | **Functional support screen.** Verify every setting affects rendered/game behavior and add color-blind/reduced-motion coverage. |
| **Handbook** | Learn concepts and rules | Search/navigation | Very broad and readable, but some claims exceed current implementation | Informational | **Useful but risky.** Generate rule facts from authoritative registries or test handbook claims against engine behavior. |

## Dominant, false and missing decisions

### Obviously dominant or exploitable

- Submit manual reports outside the weekly schedule, then repeat in the same week.
- Choose higher conviction for immediate price even when evidence/craft is worse.
- Pay agency employees the minimum possible salary.
- Use free repeatable board/manager actions.
- Re-select analysis data points for IP.
- Use batch advancement to bypass systems/costs relative to manual time.

### False or cosmetic

- Rival Counter-Bid.
- Promotion/relegation presentation.
- Hypothesis lifecycle.
- Several insight and seasonal modifier results.
- Quick interaction option differentiation.
- Gossip watch/action lifecycle.

### Important missing decisions

- Which question or hypothesis an observation is intended to test.
- Whether a new context is worth more than another look at the same context.
- Whether the report is appropriate for this audience, role, price and deadline.
- Whether to disclose uncertainty, protect a source or risk a relationship.
- Whether to update or defend a prior opinion after contradiction.
- Which client/club values the scout should prioritize when interests conflict.
- Which routine work to automate as authority grows.
- When and how to recover from a public miss or firing.
- When a career is complete and what legacy should carry forward.
